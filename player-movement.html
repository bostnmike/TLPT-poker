The Heater Meter

Who’s heating up, who’s cooling off, who’s grinding steady, and who’s riding the volatility train.

🏆 Elite 🎯 Deep 💣 Bubble ❄️ Early 🥶 Very Early 🔥 Streak
How The Heater Meter works: Each player is scored from their last 6 events using finish position, field size, knockouts, rebuys, bubbles, and clean runs.
Better finishes in larger fields help most. Knockouts and bubbles add heat. Rebuys and early exits cool the score.
More recent events carry more weight. Players qualify after 4 total events played.
