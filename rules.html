House Rules

Everything you need to know before the cards hit the felt.

Buy-in

$30 Entry

Standard buy-ins are $30.00.

Rebuys

$30 Unlimited Through Level 7

Rebuys are $30.00 and are unlimited until the end of Level 7.

Starting Stack

35K or 500K

Depending on the format, the starting stack is either 35,000 or 500,000.

Reload Rule

No Bust Required

Players do not need to bust out to rebuy. A player may reload whenever they are at or below 20% of the starting stack.

League Tracking

Stats Count Here

Bubbles are tracked for league stats, and knockouts are recorded as Hits.

RSVP

Apple Invites

Weekly attendance is handled through Apple Invites, while the site acts as the hub for league info and performance tracking.
