TLPT

League Rules

House structure, chip sets, and blind levels for both Small Blind Ante formats.

Buy-Ins

$30.00

Rebuys

$30.00 and unlimited through the end of Level 7.

Starting Stacks

40K or 500K depending on format.

Reload Rule

Players do not need to bust to rebuy. Reloads are allowed at or below 20% of the starting stack.

Level Length

All levels are 20 minutes.

Break Length

All breaks are 10 minutes.
