House Rules

Weekly game. Friendly trash talk. Real bookkeeping.

Buy-ins & Rebuys

  • Buy-ins are $30.00.
  • Rebuys are $30.00.
  • Rebuys are unlimited through the end of Level 7.
  • Players do not need to bust to rebuy.
  • Players may reload whenever they are at or below 20% of the starting stack.

Starting Stack

  • Depending on the format, the starting stack is either 35,000 or 500,000.

Game Notes

  • Payouts go to the designated finishing spots for that night’s format.
  • Bubbles are tracked for league stats.
  • Knockouts are tracked as Hits.
  • League performance is reflected throughout the site in standings, dashboards, and player profiles.

RSVP

Weekly attendance is handled through Apple Invites, with the site acting as the public hub for game information and league stats.
