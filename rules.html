TLPT Rules & Structures

1

Rebuy / Reload

If your stack is dust, or close enough to qualify as modern art, you may rebuy through the end of the final hand of Level 7, and you may reload any time you’re at or below 20% of the starting stack.

2

Structure

Whether it’s 40K on Friday or 500K on Saturday, the structure is there to give everyone a chance before the deck turns into a crime scene. Use the tables below for the full blind sheet and chip set.

3

Action

Verbal declarations are binding, string bets are not a personality trait, and one player to a hand still applies even if the Lon, Norm, or BostnMike are offering running commentary from across the table.

4

Protect Live Cards

The Commish offers a lavish selection (as long as your sports allegiances match) of card protectors for a reason, and if your cards hit the muck unguarded, that tragedy is yours. #YouveBeenWarned
